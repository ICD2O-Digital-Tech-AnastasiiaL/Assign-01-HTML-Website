However, first of all what is Cookie Run Kingdom?

Cookie Run Kingdom is a battle game with a story centered on cookies that have a great adventure, as players we join them on the journey of exploring the world of cookie run kingdom. The story develops with cookies and gives a slightly light weight of subject it explores in the story.
